    Quick Answers

    Common Questions

    How do I book a class?

    Visit our Schedule page, pick your preferred class and time, and complete the booking online. You'll receive a confirmation email instantly.

    Can I rent a board without a class?

    Absolutely! Head to our Rentals page to choose your board type and duration. Rentals are available daily from 7AM to 5PM.

    Do you offer private sessions?

    Yes! Private 1-on-1 and small group sessions are available. Contact us directly to arrange a custom session with one of our instructors.

    What is your cancellation policy?

    Cancellations made 24+ hours before class receive a full credit. Within 24 hours, bookings are non-refundable but can be rescheduled once.
