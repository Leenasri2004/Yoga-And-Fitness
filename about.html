    The Beginning

    From a Single Board to a Movement

    AquaZen was born on a quiet morning in 2014 when our founder Maya Rivers paddled out alone at sunrise and held her first warrior pose on the water. The feeling was unlike anything she'd experienced on a studio mat — the gentle rocking, the salt air, the vast horizon.

    She knew immediately that this was something the world needed. Within a year, she had gathered a small group of passionate instructors, a fleet of premium boards, and a private beach launch point on the Pacific Coast.
